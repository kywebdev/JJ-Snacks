Work Hard.
Play Hard.

Take equal parts delight and mix it with equal parts seriousness and you have a recipe for the unique flavor that is life at J&J Snacks; a recipe that has served us well for the last few decades. Maybe that’s why you’ll find (XX)% of our people have tenures with the company of 10 years or more, and more than a few with over 20. After all, you can’t make the world’s funnest snacks without having a little fun along the way. So besides hearing worlds like “funnest,” you’ll find a culture that shies away from the corporate world for one that is more akin to, well, kin. And like all kin, we constantly push, challenge and support - not just the company, but everyone that makes who we are.
